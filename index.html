Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Naar ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the app works.

1. Information We Collect

Naar does not require you to create an account or log in. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

All data generated by your use of Naar — such as health and fitness activity — is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server.

2. Notifications

Naar may request permission to send you local notifications (for example, reminders or progress alerts). These notifications are generated entirely on your device. We do not use push notification services that transmit your data to external servers.

You can enable or disable notifications at any time in your iPhone Settings.

3. Health & Fitness Data

Naar is a health and fitness app. Any health-related information you enter or that is generated within the app remains on your device. We do not access, collect, or share health data.

4. Third-Party Services

Naar does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

Naar is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
